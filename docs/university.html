University Student Projects 

MagicJellyBean is a partner and enabler of FPGA education for univerisites around the world. Here you will find Student projects using MJB boards for creative projects. The intent is to create a community of like-minded individuals who can share ideas, projects and wisdom in the FPGA and microcontroller field.
